Stories

Success Stories of Shapla Foundation Students

Most of the children selected in our program come from families struggling financially. They are deprived of many opportunities that other children get very easily. Despite the lack of opportunities, they shine bright with their conviction towards their education.

Kabir Hossain Thanks His Sponsor Tashfiq Shah

Kabir Hossain, one of our sponsored students, has successfully passed his SSC exams with an excellent GPA of 4.56 out of 5. Kabir has always shown great dedication to his studies despite many challenges at home. His achievement reflects his hard work and strong will to succeed. He is now preparing for college, ready to take on new goals and opportunities.

Umme Habiba Oishi Thanks Her Sponsor Mst. Shahina

We’re proud to share that Habiba has passed her SSC examinations with a GPA of 4.44 out of 5. Habiba has consistently shown determination in her studies, even while managing tough personal circumstances. Her result is a reflection of her perseverance and focus. As she looks forward to college life, we’re excited to see her continue to grow and achieve her dreams.

Md. Akash Ali Thanks His Sponsor Mr. Zahid

Akash’s father runs a small tea shop to maintain their family. His father’s little income from the shop did not stop Akash from pursuing his passion to study. He has been resilient throughout the challenges he faced. He has been one of the most hard working kids in our program.
